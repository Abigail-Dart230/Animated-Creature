The Night Crawler

The Night Crawler is a creature who only shows its face when the moon is bright. The eratcic creature is always searching for food to feast on.
The Night Crawler is blind on his left eye, however it makes up for this through his right eye that is constantly moving in all directions.
The bright moon enhances his right eyes's vision but on nights when the full moon shines brightest, it does the unthinkable.
The full moon will take the shape of the Night Crawler's left eye and it will be able to all that the moonlight touches.
With its large mouth, the Night Crawler will devour anything that can fit.
All creatures will hide in the night of a bright full moon.
